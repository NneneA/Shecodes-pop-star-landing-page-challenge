Tracy Chapman is a multiple-awarding winning American singer-songwriter. She is best known for her hit singles "Fast Car" and "Give Me One Reason". Her debut album, Tracy Chapman, became a commercial success, and was certified 6× Platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America.
